# Starbridge Research Plan

The Starbridge research plan studies multilingual knowledge graphs and is led by the Chenghai University AI Center. It uses BAAI/bge-m3 for embeddings, BAAI/bge-reranker-v2-m3 for reranking, and Neo4j as the graph database.

The research sample includes Chinese, English, and Spanish. Its blind evaluation contains 50 questions, and the new engine must win or tie on at least 80 percent of them.
