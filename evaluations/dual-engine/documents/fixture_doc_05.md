# Qinglan Supplier Agreement Summary

Qiming Manufacturing is the primary supplier for Project Qinglan under contract QL-2026-017. The first delivery contains 320 units, is due at Warehouse 2 in Suzhou Industrial Park, and must arrive no later than September 30, 2026.

The quality inspection sample is 8 percent. If the defect rate exceeds 2 percent, the supplier must submit a corrective-action report within 5 business days and pay all reinspection costs.
